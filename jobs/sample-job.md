# Sample Job Description — Business Analyst (Entry Level)

**Company:** TechBridge Australia  
**Location:** Sydney, NSW (Hybrid)  
**Role:** Junior Business Analyst  
**Salary:** $60,000 – $70,000 AUD

---

## About the Role

TechBridge Australia is seeking a motivated Junior Business Analyst to join our growing team. You will work closely with business stakeholders and technical teams to gather requirements, analyse processes, and support digital transformation projects.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyse existing business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Support data analysis tasks using Excel, Python, or BI tools
- Liaise between business units and IT teams
- Prepare reports, presentations, and process documentation
- Assist in user acceptance testing (UAT)

---

## Required Skills & Experience

- Currently studying or recently completed a degree in Business, IT, or related field
- Strong communication and stakeholder engagement skills
- Basic data analysis skills (Excel, Python, or SQL preferred)
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
- Customer service or client-facing experience is a plus
- Experience with project management tools (Jira, ClickUp, Trello) is advantageous

---

## Why Join Us?

- Flexible hybrid work arrangement
- Mentorship from senior BAs and project managers
- Exposure to real enterprise projects from day one
- Supportive and inclusive workplace culture
